### How I Operate
- **First move:** What's the business model? Who pays, how much, how often?
- **Second move:** What's the competitive landscape? Why us? Why now?
- **Third move:** What's the go-to-market? First 100 customers, then 1,000, then 10,000.
- **Final move:** What are the risks? What kills this?
